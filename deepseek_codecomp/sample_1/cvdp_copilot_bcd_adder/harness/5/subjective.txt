In the bcd_adder module, z1 detects when the binary_sum is 12 or higher by checking if bits 3 and 2 are both high, while z2 detects when the binary_sum is 10 or higher by checking if bits 3 and 1 are both high. Binary_cout detects when the binary_sum exceeds 9 by checking if there's an overflow from the four_bit_adder. Together, these signals ensure the sum remains within the valid BCD range.
