The RTL continuous_adder maintains accumulation in ACCUM_MODE=1 by continuously adding weighted inputs to sum_accum until either the sum reaches the window_size or triggers a verification condition. The window_size register resets sum_accum and sample_count upon reaching its value, ensuring accurate tracking within a sliding window. In contrast, when ACCUM_MODE=0, the accumulator resets on every valid data input, effectively discarding older values beyond the current window.
