In the elevator control system, `call_requests_internal` is used to maintain a record of active floor requests. When the elevator is idle, it copies incoming `call_requests` into `call_requests_internal`. While moving, new requests are added without overwriting existing ones due to bitwise OR operation. When servicing a request, the corresponding bit in `call_requests_internal` is cleared. This ensures that each floor request is processed exactly once. During both movement and idle states, efficient handling occurs as the system continuously checks `call_requests_internal` for pending requests.
