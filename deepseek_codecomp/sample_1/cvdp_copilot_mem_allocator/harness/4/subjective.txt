The `REVERSE` parameter is set to 1 in the `cvdp_leading_zero_cnt` instantiation because the `cvdp_leading_zero_cnt` module uses reverse bit ordering to count leading zeros, which aligns with the `free_slots_n` array's indexing in the `cvdp_copilot_mem_allocator` module. This ensures consistent slot allocation by treating the most significant bit of the free_slots_n array as the first available free slot, allowing accurate tracking of free slots during memory allocation and deallocation.
