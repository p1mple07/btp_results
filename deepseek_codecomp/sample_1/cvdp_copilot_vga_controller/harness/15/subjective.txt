The testbench verifies horizontal synchronization by checking the transition through active pixels (640), front porch (16 clocks), horizontal sync pulse (96 clocks), and back porch (48 clocks). It verifies vertical synchronization by checking the transition through active lines (480), front porch (10 lines), vertical sync pulse (2 lines), and back porch (33 lines). Both horizontal and vertical synchronization signals are validated against their respective timing requirements of 25 MHz pixel clock.
