The testbench ensures that race conditions or timing mismatches do not occur by first initializing all DUT instances with resetn = 0, enabling their clocks, and setting their input parameters. Since each DUT operates on its own clock edge and has its own out_val and done signals, the testbench can monitor their states independently. The testbench also uses a structured approach to running each test sequence, resetting the necessary signals and waiting for each DUT to complete before moving to the next. Additionally, the clock period is long enough (10ns) to allow each DUT to process its inputs and produce outputs without overlap.
