In the `bcd_adder` module, the signals `z1`, `z2`, and `binary_cout` detect three specific result ranges by analyzing the `binary_sum` bits:
1. `z1` checks if the third and second bits (`binary_sum[3]` and `binary_sum[2]`) are both high, indicating a sum >= 12.
2. `z2` checks if the third and first bits (`binary_sum[3]` and `binary_sum[1]`) are both high, indicating a sum between 8 and 11.
3. `binary_cout` captures any carry-out from the most significant bit addition, signaling an overflow beyond the BCD range.
