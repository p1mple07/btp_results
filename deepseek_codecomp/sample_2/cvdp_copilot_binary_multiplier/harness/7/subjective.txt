The bit-width of `partial_products` is chosen as `[2*WIDTH-1:0]` to ensure that all shifted values of `B` fit within the allocated space without overflowing. Each partial product from shifting `B` by `i` positions requires exactly `WIDTH + i` bits, but since `i` ranges up to `WIDTH-1`, the maximum needed is `2*WIDTH-1` bits. This allocation allows each shifted value to be fully represented before summation.
