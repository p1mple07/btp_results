The `BST_SUB_TREE` module is required to construct the binary search tree under test, providing a structured way to initialize the BST with specific data. It separates the process of BST generation from the search operation, ensuring clarity and modularity. During testing, the `BST_SUB_TREE` builds the BST using the provided input data, and the search is performed on this pre-built structure. This integration ensures that the search module operates on a valid BST, maintaining consistency and stability across tests by synchronizing their clocks and resets.
