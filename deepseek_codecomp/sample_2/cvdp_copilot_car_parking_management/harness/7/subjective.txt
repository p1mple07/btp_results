The simulate_entry task holds the vehicle_entry_sensor signal high for 10ns to ensure the event is fully registered by the FSM during the next clock cycle. Since the FSM operates on a 100MHz clock (10ns per cycle), waiting for 10ns allows the state machine to process the new state transition correctly. If the hold time were shorter than 10ns, it might not allow the FSM to complete its processing, leading to incorrect state transitions. Conversely, making the hold time longer than 10ns could cause unnecessary delays and potential state inconsistencies.
