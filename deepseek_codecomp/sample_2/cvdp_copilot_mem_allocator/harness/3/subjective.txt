When both `release_en` and `acquire_en` are asserted simultaneously:

1. If `release_addr` and `acquire_addr` are the same, the system attempts to release and acquire from the same address, resulting in no meaningful change to the free slots and potentially inconsistent state due to concurrent access.

2. If `release_addr` and `acquire_addr` are different, the system may incorrectly allow both actions to proceed, leading to a possible conflict in managing the free slots.
