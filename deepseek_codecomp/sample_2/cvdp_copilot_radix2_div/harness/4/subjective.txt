The randomized testing section of the testbench verifies the correctness of the radix2_div module. Here's how it works:

1. Generates random 8-bit values for dividend and divisor while ensuring divisor ≠ 0.
2. Applies these values to the radix2_div module by connecting the appropriate signals.
3. Waits for the division operation to complete (i.e., when done becomes 1).
4. Computes expected quotient and remainder using dividend / divisor and dividend % divisor respectively.
5. Compares actual quotient and remainder with expected values and reports pass/fail results.
