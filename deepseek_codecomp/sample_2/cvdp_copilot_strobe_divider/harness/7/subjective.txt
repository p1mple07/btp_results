When the In_Ratio is set to 0, the strobe_divider module asserts the Out_Valid signal for every valid In_Valid pulse, effectively bypassing the division logic. This occurs because the condition (In_Ratio == 0) inside the ratio counter logic directly sets r_next_OutValid to 1 whenever In_Ratio is 0. As a result, the module functions as a direct-through path from In to Out without any delay or counting. The interaction with Out_Ready ensures that once Out_Ready is asserted, Out_Valid remains valid until the receiver acknowledges readiness, thus maintaining consistent timing integrity.
