The `reg_gpio_dir` and `module_active` signals work together to determine the bidirectional behavior of the GPIO pins. `reg_gpio_dir` configures each pin as an input or output based on its address, while `module_active` gates the APB interface activity. During power-down mode, `module_active` disables the APB interface, preventing unintended interactions with the APB bus. The combined use ensures proper isolation and prevents data corruption. Potential issues arise when external signals interfere with the APB interface during power-down, requiring careful initialization and stabilization processes. By separating configuration from operation, this design provides flexibility and robustness in managing GPIO behavior under varying conditions.
