The testbench ensures that race conditions and timing mismatches are avoided by enabling each DUT instance sequentially for testing, rather than simultaneously. By configuring and enabling each DUT one at a time, the testbench prevents any overlap or competition for clock edges, ensuring accurate monitoring of out_val and done signals. Each DUT operates independently based on its specific configuration, and the testbench tracks the progress of each DUT separately to validate its behavior without interference from other instances.
