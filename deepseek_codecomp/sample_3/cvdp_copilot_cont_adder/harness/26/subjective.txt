In ACCUM_MODE=1, the system accumulates data periodically upon reaching the specified window_size, resetting sum_accum and sample_count after each full cycle. When data_valid is True, sample_count increases sequentially. Upon reaching window_size, sum_ready is set to 1, triggering the clearing of sum_accum and sample_count. In contrast, ACCUM_MODE=0 uses threshold checks to determine sum readiness without periodic resets based on window_size.
