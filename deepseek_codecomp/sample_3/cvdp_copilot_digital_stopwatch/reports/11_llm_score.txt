When the start_stop signal is asserted to pause the stopwatch, the counters for seconds, minutes, and hours are locked in their current state. The one_sec_pulse signal is cleared, preventing any further increments until the pause is lifted by asserting start_stop again. This allows the user to manually stop the stopwatch without affecting the counted time. Once resumed, the counters begin incrementing again as normal.
