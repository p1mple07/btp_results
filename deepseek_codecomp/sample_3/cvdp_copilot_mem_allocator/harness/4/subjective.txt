The `REVERSE` parameter is set to 1 in the `cvdp_leading_zero_cnt` instantiation to enable the leading zero counter to correctly process the free slots in the `free_slots_n` register. In the `cvdp_copilot_mem_allocator`, each bit in `free_slots_n` corresponds to a specific free slot address. Setting `REVERSE` to 1 ensures that the leading zero counter processes these bits in the correct order, matching the actual address hierarchy. This alignment is crucial for accurately determining the number of contiguous free slots in memory.
