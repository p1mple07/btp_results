In the bcd_adder module, the signals z1, z2, and binary_cout detect three specific result ranges to ensure the sum stays within the BCD range. z1 checks if the sum's highest two bits (bits 3 and 2) are set, indicating a value greater than or equal to 8. z2 checks if the sum's highest and middle bits (bits 3 and 1) are set, indicating a value greater than or equal to 10. binary_cout detects if there is an overflow beyond the BCD range by checking the carry-out from the four_bit_adder.
