When ACCUM_MODE is set to 1, the system accumulates weighted inputs until either the sum reaches or exceeds a specified threshold (THRESHOLD_VALUE_1 or THRESHOLD_VALUE_2) or the number of samples reaches the window_size. Once either condition is met, the accumulated sum is stored in sum_out and avg_out, while sum_accum and sample_count are reset to zero. This mechanism allows the system to maintain a running total but also periodically resets it upon meeting specific criteria.
