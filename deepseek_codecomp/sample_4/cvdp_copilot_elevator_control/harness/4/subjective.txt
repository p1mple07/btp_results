The elevator control system uses `call_requests_internal` to maintain an internal copy of active floor requests. It integrates new requests by updating `call_requests_internal`, clears serviced requests by resetting their status in `call_requests_internal`, and prevents duplicates by ensuring each floor's request is processed only once before being removed from `call_requests_internal`. During movement, it prioritizes the highest or lowest request based on direction, ensuring efficient handling. During idle state, it processes all pending requests sequentially, optimizing resource utilization.
