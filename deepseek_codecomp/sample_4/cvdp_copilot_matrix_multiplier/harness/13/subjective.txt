The output signal's latency in the matrix_multiplier module consists of three stages: multiplication, accumulation, and output. During the multiplication stage, each individual element calculation takes one clock cycle. The accumulation stage adds these products sequentially across all columns of matrix A, requiring COL_A clock cycles to complete. Finally, the output stage writes the accumulated result to the output matrix, which introduces an additional 2 clock cycles due to the valid_out_reg signaling mechanism. Thus, the total latency is the sum of these three stages, resulting in a latency of COL_A + 2 clock cycles.
