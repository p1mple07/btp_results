The matrix_c_stage variable represents a temporary storage area where intermediate results of the matrix multiplication are computed and accumulated. During the computation of matrix C, the stage is first populated with individual element-wise products of matrices A and B. As the loops progress, these intermediate values are added together to form the partial sums required for each element of the resulting matrix C. Once all computations are complete, the accumulated values in matrix_c_stage are assigned to the final output matrix C.
