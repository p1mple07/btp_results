The pipeline_mac module preserves the accuracy of its final accumulated result by using registers to buffer the intermediate multiplication and accumulation results during periods when the input valid signal (valid_i) is low. In the multiplication stage, the mult_result_reg remains unchanged when valid_i is low, preventing any invalid multiplication results from being stored. Similarly, in the accumulation stage, the accumulation_reg does not update until valid_i returns to high, ensuring that the accumulator only adds valid partial products. Additionally, the counter and valid_out signals are updated only when valid_i is active-high, maintaining the integrity of the accumulated result throughout the pipelining process.
