The `reg_gpio_dir` and `module_active` signals ensure correct operation by controlling the direction of each GPIO pin based on the stored configuration and powering them appropriately during inactive periods. During power-down, `module_active` disables output signals through `reg_power_down`, preventing unintended activity. However, changes to `reg_gpio_dir` during power-down may not take effect immediately, potentially causing instability. Additionally, improper handling of the power-down transition could lead to temporary signal issues.
