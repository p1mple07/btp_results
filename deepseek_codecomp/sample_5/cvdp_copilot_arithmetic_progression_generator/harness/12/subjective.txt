The testbench ensures that race conditions or timing mismatches do not occur by maintaining synchronization across all DUT instances through the shared clock signal. Each DUT instance is configured with its own DATA_WIDTH, SEQUENCE_LENGTH, start_val, and step_size, but they all operate under the same clock timing. The testbench utilizes independent tasks for each sequence, allowing each DUT to progress without interference from others. The task monitors the out_val and done signals for each DUT separately, ensuring accurate state tracking. Additionally, the testbench employs careful logging and assertions to validate the behavior of each DUT, preventing timing mismatches and race conditions.
