In the `bcd_adder` module, the signals `z1`, `z2`, and `binary_cout` detect specific result ranges by analyzing particular bits of `binary_sum`. `z1` checks if bits 3 and 2 of `binary_sum` are both high, indicating a sum of 12 or higher. `z2` checks if bits 3 and 1 of `binary_sum` are both high, indicating a sum of 10 or 11. `binary_cout` is set if there is an overflow beyond the BCD range, typically when the sum equals 10. Together, these signals ensure detection of sums outside the valid BCD range (0-9).
