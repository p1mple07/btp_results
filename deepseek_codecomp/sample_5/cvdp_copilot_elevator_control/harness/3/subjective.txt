In the elevator control system, the FSM transitions from the IDLE state to either MOVING_UP or MOVING_DOWN based on the comparison between the current floor and the maximum (max_request) or minimum (min_request) requested floor. If the current floor is below the lowest requested floor (min_request), the elevator moves down (MOVING_DOWN). Conversely, if the current floor is above the highest requested floor (max_request), the elevator moves up (MOVING_UP). This decision ensures efficient handling of requests by prioritizing the farthest floor in the specified direction, thus avoiding unnecessary back-and-forth movements and optimizing the elevator's operational efficiency.
