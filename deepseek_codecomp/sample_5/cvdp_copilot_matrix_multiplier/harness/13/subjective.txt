The output signal latency in the matrix_multiplier module is determined by three stages: multiplication, accumulation, and output. During each clock cycle, the multiplication stage computes the product of matrix elements, while the accumulation stage sums these products. The output stage then stores the final result. The total latency of COL_A + 2 clock cycles arises because the validity signal must propagate through COL_A stages before being applied to the accumulation and output stages, with an additional two cycles for the output storage.
