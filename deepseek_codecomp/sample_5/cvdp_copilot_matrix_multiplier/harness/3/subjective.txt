The three major stages of contribution of matrix_c_stage in the matrix_multiplier RTL code are:
1. Initialization stage: Where the initial product terms of matrix elements are loaded into matrix_c_stage.
2. Accumulation stage: Where the intermediate results are accumulated by adding new product terms to the existing ones in matrix_c_stage.
3. Final assignment stage: Where the accumulated intermediate results are written into the final output matrix matrix_c.
