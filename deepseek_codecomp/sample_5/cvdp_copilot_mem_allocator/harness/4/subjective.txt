The `REVERSE` parameter is set to `1` in the `cvdp_leading_zero_cnt` instantiation to ensure that the leading zero counting operates in the reverse order compared to the default case. This is necessary because the free slots in the `cvdp_copilot_mem_allocator` are managed in a way that requires the leading zero count to follow a specific slot allocation pattern. By reversing the bit order, the leading zero count accurately reflects the free slot positions, ensuring proper memory allocation and deallocation functionality.
