The testbench verifies horizontal synchronization by setting a 640-pixel active region, followed by a 16-cycle front porch, a 96-cycle sync pulse, and a 48-cycle back porch, matching the 640x480 VGA specification. Vertical synchronization is verified by ensuring a 480-line active region, followed by a 10-line front porch, a 2-line sync pulse, and a 33-line back porch, also aligning with the VGA timing standards.
