## Design Specification:

A **barrel shifter** is a digital circuit that shifts a data word by a specified number of bits in one clock cycle. It is commonly used in digital systems like microprocessors to perform rapid shifts of binary numbers, which is useful in operations such as multiplication, division, and data manipulation.
