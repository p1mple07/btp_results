In the binary_multiplier module, the partial_products array is defined with a width of 2*WIDTH-1 bits to accommodate the maximum shift of B, which can be shifted up to WIDTH-1 positions. This ensures that even when B is shifted left by its maximum amount, the resulting value fits within 2*WIDTH-1 bits, thus preventing overflow. By using this bit-width, all possible shifted values of B are correctly stored, ensuring proper addition of partial products to produce the final multiplication result.
