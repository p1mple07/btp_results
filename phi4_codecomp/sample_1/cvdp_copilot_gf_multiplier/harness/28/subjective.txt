The testbench uses an invalid width of 34 bits to verify that the gf_mac module correctly detects and handles cases where the input width is not a multiple of 8. With an invalid width, the module sets error_flag high and valid_result low, ensuring that no spurious computation occurs and that the result is forced to 0. This test is critical for system-level validation because it confirms that the design gracefully handles improper configurations, thereby preventing potential errors from propagating in larger systems.
