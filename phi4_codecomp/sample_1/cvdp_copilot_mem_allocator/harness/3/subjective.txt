If both release_en and acquire_en are asserted simultaneously, the module first sets the free slot at release_addr to 1 and then sets the slot at acquire_addr_r to 0; thus, if release_addr equals acquire_addr, the release is effectively canceled by the subsequent acquisition, leaving that slot allocated. If the addresses are different, one slot is released (set to free) while another distinct slot is acquired (set to allocated), resulting in a net change in the free_slots register.
