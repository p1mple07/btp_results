The REVERSE parameter set to 1 ensures that the nibble ordering in cvdp_leading_zero_cnt is maintained in its natural order, which is crucial for correctly interpreting the free_slots_n register in the cvdp_copilot_mem_allocator. This natural ordering allows the module to accurately count the number of leading zeros in free_slots_n, thereby determining the lowest-index free slot available for allocation. In essence, by not reversing the nibble order, the design guarantees that the slot allocation method works as intended, with cvdp_leading_zero_cnt providing the correct index for memory allocation.
