If DATA_WIDTH is not divisible by 8, the reversal loops in the testbench will only process the first complete multiple of 8 bits, leaving any remaining bits unswizzled. This means that expected_data_out will have only the first (DATA_WIDTH/8)*8 bits correctly reversed while the extra bits (DATA_WIDTH mod 8) remain unprocessed or incorrectly assigned. Consequently, the verification may report errors since the expected output does not fully match the actual output when DATA_WIDTH is not a multiple of 8.
