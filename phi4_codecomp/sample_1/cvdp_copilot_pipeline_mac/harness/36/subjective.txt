The design preserves accuracy by capturing the multiplication result in a register (mult_result_reg) as soon as valid_i is high, ensuring that even if valid_i goes low, the computed value is not lost. In the accumulation stage, the intermediate accumulation register (accumulation_reg) retains the previous value and adds the stored multiplication result only when the valid signal (valid_i_s1) is high, so temporary gaps in valid_i do not affect the ongoing sum. The pipeline stages and the use of intermediate valid signal registers (valid_out_s0, valid_out_s1, valid_out_s2) ensure that once the data is captured, it is correctly forwarded and accumulated until the final valid output is generated, thereby preserving the integrity of the final accumulated result.
