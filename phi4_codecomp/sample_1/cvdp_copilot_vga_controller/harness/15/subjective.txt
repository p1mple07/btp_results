The testbench tb_vga_controller simulates a 25 MHz clock and applies a reset to initialize the VGA controller, then uses cycle_count and line_count counters to emulate the horizontal and vertical timings defined by the 640 x 480 VGA specification (640 active pixels with 16 front porch, 96 sync pulse, and 48 back porch clocks for horizontal timing; 480 active lines with 10 front porch, 2 sync pulse, and 33 back porch lines for vertical timing). It prints messages at the boundaries of each phase, thereby verifying that the hsync and vsync signals are asserted or deasserted at the correct intervals according to the standard. Additionally, by continuously updating the color input and observing the outputs during the active display periods, the testbench confirms that valid pixel data is produced only during the active regions, ensuring full conformance with the VGA specification.
