In the write_buffer_merge module, the merged_data signal serves as an accumulator that concatenates successive write data inputs into a single, wider data bus, effectively merging multiple writes into one output. With each incoming write, the new data is shifted in at the left end of merged_data while the previous data is shifted right by the width of the input data.
