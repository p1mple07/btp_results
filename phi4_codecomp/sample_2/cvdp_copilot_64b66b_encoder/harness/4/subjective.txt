When encoder_control_in changes from 8'b00000000 to 8'b00000001 during operation, the module will, on the next clock edge, update the sync_word to 2'b10 and clear encoded_data to 64'b0, indicating a switch to control encoding mode. This change means that the module is no longer passing the data directly from encoder_data_in, but rather preparing for a control word. Consequently, the immediate effect is that sync_word now signals a control header while encoded_data is reset to zero.
