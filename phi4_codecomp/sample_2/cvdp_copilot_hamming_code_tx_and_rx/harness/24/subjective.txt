In the golden_hamming_tx function, the transmitted data is constructed so that parity bits occupy positions that are powers of two while the data bits are placed in the remaining positions; this means that bit 0 and the most significant bit (ENCODED_DATA) are not used for meaningful data. Consequently, the $urandom_range is limited to 1 through ENCODED_DATA-1 to ensure that the error injection targets only the valid bits that carry either data or parity information.
