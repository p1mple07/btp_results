The matrix_multiplier module achieves 100% throughput by fully pipelining its operations so that a new multiplication can begin every clock cycle. It registers intermediate multiplication results in the mult_stage array, allowing the computation to be overlapped across cycles. The valid_out_reg register shifts the valid_in signal, ensuring that each new input is processed in a new pipeline stage. When the addition stage is used, it further processes partial sums in a pipelined fashion without introducing stalls. Consequently, by managing intermediate results through dedicated registers and pipelined stages, the design maintains a steady 100% throughput.
