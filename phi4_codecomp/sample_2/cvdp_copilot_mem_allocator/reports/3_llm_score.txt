Response:
When both release_en and acquire_en are asserted with the same address, the release operation sets that slot free but the subsequent acquire operation immediately re-allocates it, effectively canceling the release. When the addresses are different, the release operation frees one slot while the acquire operation allocates a different slot, so the overall allocation count remains unchanged.
