The module uses pipeline registers to store the intermediate multiplication result in mult_result_reg, ensuring that once a valid multiplication is computed, the result is preserved even if valid_i goes low in subsequent cycles. In the accumulation stage, the design updates the accumulation register only when a valid input is present, thereby retaining the previously computed partial sums until valid_i is reasserted. Additionally, the pipelining mechanism and state-holding registers (such as valid_out_s1 and valid_out_s2) synchronize the stages, ensuring that the final accumulated result remains accurate despite intermittent low valid_i signals.
