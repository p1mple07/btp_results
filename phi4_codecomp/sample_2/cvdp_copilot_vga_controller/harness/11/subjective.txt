The horizontal sync pulse is implemented in the H_PULSE_STATE of the horizontal state machine. In this state, the HSYNC signal (hsync_reg) is driven LOW. The relevant block is:

H_PULSE_STATE: begin
    h_counter <= (h_counter == H_PULSE) ? 10'd0 : (h_counter + 10'd1);
    hsync_reg <= LOW;
    h_state <= (h_counter == H_PULSE) ? H_BACK_STATE : H_PULSE_STATE;
end
