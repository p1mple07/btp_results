The mask signal complicates validation in mode 11 because it applies a bitwise AND after the shift operation, altering the straightforward relationship between the input and the shifted result. The testbench verifies its correctness by setting up specific input vectors that include a defined mask, executing the shift operation, and then comparing the output with a pre-calculated expected value. This validation is critical to ensure that the masked shift functionality is implemented correctly, preventing errors in designs where only selected bits should be modified.
