In ACCUM_MODE=1, the continuous_adder accumulates the weighted input into the sum_accum register while incrementing the sample_count register with each valid data input. The design compares the incremented sample_count (sample_count + 1) against the window_size parameter to determine when a sufficient number of samples have been collected. Once the condition (sample_count + 1 >= window_size) is met, the sum_ready_reg flag is set to indicate that the accumulation is complete and the output is ready. At this point, the design outputs the final sum and average, then resets both sum_accum and sample_count to zero to start a new accumulation cycle.
