The counter starts at 0 and wraps around to 1023 on the first decrement in BINARY_DOWN mode. Since the counter decrements by 1 on each enable cycle, it must undergo 1008 enable cycles to reach 15 (10'b0000001111) from 1023. Given that enable_in is high for 10 cycles out of every 20 cycles, the total number of clock cycles required is 1008 * 2 = 2016 cycles. At a 50 MHz clock frequency, 2016 cycles take 2016/50,000,000 seconds, which is approximately 40.32 microseconds.
