The multiplication stage registers the product in mult_result_reg so that even if valid_i goes low, the computed multiplication result is preserved until it is needed. The accumulation stage only updates the accumulator when a valid input is detected via the pipelined valid signal (valid_i_s1), ensuring that no spurious or zero values are added during cycles when valid_i is low. This careful use of pipeline registers and conditional update logic ensures that transient low valid signals do not disrupt the accumulation process, thereby preserving the accuracy of the final result.
