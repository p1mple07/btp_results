The comparison between the Golden Model and the DUT outputs is described in the 'Output Verification' section of the Test Methodology. In that section, it is explained that the testbench compares the DUT outputs (I and Q) to the expected values computed by the reference (Golden) model, logs any mismatches (including the index, DUT value, and expected value), and maintains counts of total, passed, and failed tests. This process is further detailed in the 'Golden Model Verification' section of the Test Strategy, which outlines the steps of applying inputs, computing the expected outputs, performing the comparison, and summarizing the results.
