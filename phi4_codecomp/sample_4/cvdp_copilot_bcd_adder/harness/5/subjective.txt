In the bcd_adder module, the signal z1 is generated by ANDing the most significant bit (MSB) and the second MSB of binary_sum, indicating that the result is in the 8–15 range and may be outside the valid BCD range. The signal z2 is created by ANDing the MSB with the third bit of binary_sum, which further refines the detection to identify when the result falls between 10 and 15. Finally, the binary_cout from the initial addition, combined with z1 and z2, is used to set the final cout signal, triggering the necessary correction to bring the result back into the valid BCD range.
