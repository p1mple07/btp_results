The subsequent stages are registered to break up the critical path, ensuring that the overall delay is governed by the pipelined stages rather than the first stage. Therefore, using REG = 8'b11111110 minimizes the initial latency, reducing the total time to produce a valid output compared to REG = 8'b11111111, where an unnecessary register at the first stage adds extra delay.
