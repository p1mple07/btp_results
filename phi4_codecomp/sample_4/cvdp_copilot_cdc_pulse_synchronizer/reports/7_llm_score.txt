Reference:
A pulse signal in the source clock domain (`src_pulse`) is a single-cycle event. A short pulse can be easily missed if the destination clock domain (`des_clock`) is slower, as it may not detect the pulse within its sampling window. To solve this, the toggle flop circuit converts each incoming pulse into a **toggle (or flip)** of a persistent signal, `pls_toggle`, which holds its state until it is detected and processed by the slower clock domain.

As a result, the synchronizer can accurately detect and transfer the pulse while maintaining signal integrity.
