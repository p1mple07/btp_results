Section 1: Delta Updates

The weight (delta_w) and bias (delta_b) updates are computed based on the error and the learning rate. The specification states:

"3. Delta Updates

The weight (delta_w) and bias (delta_b) updates are computed based on the error and the learning rate:

Delta w = LEARNING_RATE * error * x_in

Delta b = LEARNING_RATE * error

These updates are calculated combinationally."

Section 2: Weight and Bias Updates

The weight (w) and bias (b) registers are updated sequentially at the rising edge of the clock (clk), and can be reset asynchronously using the reset signal. The specification states:

"4. Weight and Bias Updates

The weight (w) and bias (b) registers are updated sequentially at the rising edge of the clock (clk), and can be reset asynchronously using the reset signal:

w <- w + delta_w

b <- b + delta_b

If the reset signal is asserted, both w and b are reset to 0."
