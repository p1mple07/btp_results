In ACCUM_MODE=1, the continuous_adder module accumulates the weighted input signals in the sum_accum register while incrementing the sample_count register for each valid data sample.
It continuously checks if the incremented sample_count meets or exceeds the window_size parameter to determine if the accumulation window is complete.
Once the condition (sample_count + 1 >= window_size) is met, the module outputs the accumulated sum and computes the average by dividing the sum by window_size, then resets both sum_accum and sample_count to zero.
Thus, window_size acts as a threshold that triggers the final output generation and resets the counters for a new accumulation cycle.
