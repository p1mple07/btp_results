The introduction of the i_pause signal in Test 4 simulates an interruption in data transfer by halting the normal progression of data output. By asserting i_pause during transmission, the testbench forces the design to pause, allowing it to verify that the current state and buffered data are maintained without further progression. Once i_pause is deasserted after a controlled delay, the design resumes data transmission from the correct point, ensuring continuity and proper sequencing. This exercise validates that the design robustly handles interruptions, confirming its ability to manage temporary halts without compromising data integrity or synchronization.
