If a value less than 6 is chosen for WIDTH, the testbench will generate 6-bit wide signed signals while the DUT operates with a narrower bit-width, causing the input values to be truncated or misrepresented, which in turn leads to incorrect GCD computations. As a result, the expected outputs computed by the testbench will not match the actual DUT outputs, causing the testbench to report failures due to the bit-width mismatch and potential overflow or truncation issues.
