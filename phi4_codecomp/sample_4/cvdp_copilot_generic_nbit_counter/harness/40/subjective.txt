In BINARY_DOWN mode, the counter decrements by 1 on each enable pulse starting from 10'b0000000000. Since the target output is 10'b0000001111 (15 in decimal), the counter must decrement 1009 times because (1024 - 1009) equals 15. Given that the enable signal is active for one cycle every 10 cycles, the counter updates once every 10 cycles, resulting in a total of 1009 * 10 = 10090 cycles. At a 50 MHz clock (20 ns per cycle), this corresponds to 10090 * 20 ns, which is approximately 201.8 microseconds.
