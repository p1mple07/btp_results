The REVERSE parameter set to 1 in the cvdp_leading_zero_cnt instantiation reverses the nibble order during the leading zero count, ensuring that the count aligns with the bit ordering of the free_slots_n register used for slot allocation in the cvdp_copilot_mem_allocator. In the allocator, free_slots_n is updated to mark free and allocated slots, and the cvdp_leading_zero_cnt module determines the index of the first available slot by counting the number of leading zeros. This reversal is essential because it correctly interprets the bit order in free_slots_n, thereby ensuring accurate identification and allocation of the next free slot.
