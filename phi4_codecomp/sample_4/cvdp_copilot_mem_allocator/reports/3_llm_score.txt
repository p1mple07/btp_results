If the acquire and release addresses are the same (i.e., the first available slot is the one that is just released), `free_slots_n[acquire_addr_r]` will overwrite `free_slots_n[release_addr]` resulting in the slot being allocated despite being marked for release in the same cycle.
If the acquire and release addresses are different, both `free_slots_n[acquire_addr_r]` and `free_slots_n[release_addr]` are updated independently and one slot is allocated while another is released within the same cycle.


Response:
When both signals are asserted and the release and acquire addresses are the same, the slot is first released (set to 1) and then acquired (set to 0), effectively resulting in the slot being allocated. When the addresses are different, the release operation marks the release address as free while the acquire operation marks the acquire address as allocated, so both operations occur concurrently without conflict.
