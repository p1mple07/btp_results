In ACCUM_MODE==1, the module accumulates the weighted input into the sum_accum register and increments the sample_count on every valid data input. The window_size parameter is used as a threshold to determine when the accumulation window is complete, specifically when sample_count + 1 reaches or exceeds window_size. Once the condition is met, the module computes the final sum and average (by dividing the accumulated sum by window_size) and then resets both sum_accum and sample_count to zero for the next window. This design ensures that the accumulator processes data in fixed-size windows, allowing for controlled averaging and timely resets.
