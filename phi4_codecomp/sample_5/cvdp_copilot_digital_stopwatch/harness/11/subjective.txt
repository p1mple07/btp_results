When the start_stop signal is active, the module continuously increments a counter and generates a one-second pulse when the counter reaches its maximum value. If the start_stop signal is deasserted midway through counting, the counter stops incrementing and the one_sec_pulse signal is cleared, effectively suppressing the pulse. Without the one_sec_pulse, the always block that updates the seconds, minutes, and hour counters does not trigger, so the displayed time remains unchanged. Additionally, the running flag is cleared, indicating that the stopwatch is paused until the start_stop signal is reasserted.
