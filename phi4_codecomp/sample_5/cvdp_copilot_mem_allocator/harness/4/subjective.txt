In the cvdp_copilot_mem_allocator, the free_slots_n register tracks memory slot availability by marking free slots with a '1' and allocated slots with a '0'. The cvdp_leading_zero_cnt module is used to count the number of leading zeros in free_slots_n, thereby identifying the index of the first available memory slot for allocation. Setting the REVERSE parameter to 1 configures the nibble processing logic to operate in the natural bit order of free_slots_n, ensuring that the leading zero count accurately reflects the intended slot allocation order.
