If both signals are asserted simultaneously and the release_addr and the previous cycle’s acquire_addr (used in the combinational block) refer to the same slot, the acquire operation will override the release operation, resulting in the slot being marked as allocated (0) rather than free (1). If they refer to different slots, then the module will mark the slot specified by release_addr as free while the slot indicated by the previous cycle’s acquire_addr is marked allocated, thereby performing both operations concurrently on separate slots.
