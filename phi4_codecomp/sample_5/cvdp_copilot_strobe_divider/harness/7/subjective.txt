When In_Ratio is set to 0, the module bypasses its division logic and asserts Out_Valid for every valid input pulse, effectively removing any counting mechanism. This configuration ensures that the output pulse is generated immediately upon receiving an In_Valid signal without any delay or accumulation. However, the backpressure mechanism remains active, so if Out_Ready is deasserted, the Out_Valid signal will be held high until Out_Ready becomes asserted. Additionally, the latency parameter (Latency_g) determines whether Out_Valid is updated immediately or after one clock cycle, but the fundamental behavior of asserting Out_Valid on every valid pulse remains unchanged when In_Ratio is 0.
