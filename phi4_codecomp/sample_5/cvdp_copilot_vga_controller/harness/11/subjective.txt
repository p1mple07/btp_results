The horizontal sync pulse is driven LOW in the H_PULSE_STATE block of the always_ff process. During this state, the code assigns hsync_reg = LOW. Here is the relevant block:

                H_PULSE_STATE: begin
                    h_counter <= (h_counter == H_PULSE) ? 10'd0 : (h_counter + 10'd1);
                    hsync_reg <= LOW;
                    h_state <= (h_counter == H_PULSE) ? H_BACK_STATE : H_PULSE_STATE;
                end

This block ensures that the HSYNC signal is driven LOW during the horizontal pulse period.
