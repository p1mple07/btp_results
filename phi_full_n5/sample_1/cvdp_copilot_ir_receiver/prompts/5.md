#### **States in the State Machine**

1. **IDLE**:

2. **START**:

   - On successful detection, transition to the `DECODING` state; otherwise, transition back to `IDLE` on failure.

3. **DECODING**:
   - Decodes the 12-bit data by detecting each bit’s HIGH and LOW timing.
   - Distinguishes between `0` and `1` based on pulse widths. When all 12 bits are decoded, transitions to the `FINISH` state.

4. **FINISH**:
   - Extract the 12-bit IR Frame from the received IR signal.
   - Asserts the valid signal and outputs the decoded data. Then transitions to the `FRAME_SPACE` state.

5. **FRAME_SPACE**:
   - Monitors the 45 ms frame space. If the space duration completes, transition back to `IDLE`.
